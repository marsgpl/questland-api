Event tactics

Reforge Guild

  • Keep skulls reward ‘till the last day
  • Don’t trade/give away boss gifts tickets, your team leader will tell you how to trade them
  • Sunday evening / monday morning you have to communicate to your team leader your number of gifts (red, yellow, blue) and your multiplicator
  • Farm event tickets and gifts in campaign
  • Try not to waste free event tickets and craft everyday tickets
  • Try to buy all daily available gift boss tickets in event shop
  • Use 1 ticket per guild boss. Even if you made 0 damage, you will get full reward.
